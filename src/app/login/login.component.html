             Attorney Registeration: Attorneys will be registere by Federal Law Board.
             Registered Agents: All Registered Agents will be registered by state of Delware.
             Defendants: All Defendants willl be registered by their respective Registered Agent.

All legal documents movement and acknowledgement across parties will happen only on digital platform!

Flow of Demand Letter

  1. Plaintiff's attorney fills the required information about defendant and attach demand letter and submmit.
  2. The data is stored on blockchian and demand letter copy is stored in IPFS.
  3. The data and demand letter sent to registered agent i.e CSC so that they can transfer it to Defendant.
  4. Demand letter is sent to defendant to Acknowledge.
